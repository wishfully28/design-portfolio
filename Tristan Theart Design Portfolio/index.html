About Me

Commencing my exploration into the realm of graphic design during my high school years, I undertook a self-taught journey driven by an early fascination with technology and design. To formalize and enhance my skills, I successfully completed an Adobe and CIW certified Graphic Design Essentials course in 2020. This educational milestone significantly broadened my artistic horizons, providing exposure to various traditional and visual art forms.

During the course, I actively contributed to my school newspaper by developing advertisements, offering valuable insights into the methodical aspects of design. This experience not only refined my skills but also instilled a disciplined approach to the creative process.

Post-course completion, I transitioned into practical application, accumulating hands-on experience in diverse roles as a designer. Notable contributions include providing design support to my local municipality and serving as a social media content creator for esports in South Africa. These engagements spanned both public and private organizations, enriching my professional repertoire.

My creative focus has been on continuous experimentation with various art styles, emphasizing rapid communication, advertising, and digital illustration. This strategic emphasis aligns with my commitment to staying at the forefront of design trends and effectively communicating messages through visual mediums.
